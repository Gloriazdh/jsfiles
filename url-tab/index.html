2017 Forum on China-France Investment Dialogue

Back
Share:  分享到：
  • Printer Friendly Page

In recent years, the world’s second largest economy, China is obviously increasing its overseas investment. Meanwhile, Chinese companies are investing more and more in France, and into an ever broader range of French flagship industries, such as energy, equipment manufacturing, tourism, telecoms and agriculture, etc. French companies are eager to understand what lies behind this eagerness. To understand the reasons, features and outlook concerning overseas investment by Chinese companies, it is crucial to first comprehend the national strategy for the “Belt and Road” strategic initiative, which provides guidance for Chinese investors. Participating Chinese experts and scholars will first explain the meaning and purpose behind this strategy and its implications on Chinese investors’ investment initiatives.

CEIBS, which is a top-class business school in Asia and one of the most influential economic think tanks and elite training institutions in China, is co-organizing the 2017 Forum on China-France Investment Dialogue with the CCI Paris Ile-de-France which is the largest chamber of commerce and industry in Europe, and with the CCICF. This will feed into discussions on how to construct win-win foundations, making this event a unique platform for discussion between active players in the investment field from both countries.

Language: French/Chinese

* The Forum Organizing Committee will review your registration and send out a formal confirmation letter once it has been approved.
